What is Data Sense?

Data Sense is a utility that allows users to share documents and files with other people. We took a simple approach with this app to create a simpe work environtment making it seamless as possible and easy to use.

Why did we make this app?

This is our senior project at Luther College. Sage was approached by his father to develop an evironment that allows people to come together to share data for others to share. This application would be used by the Government of the Marshall Islands. They simply needed an easy way to access data from other ministries.
